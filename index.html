How It Works

From Scope to Signal in Three Steps

STEP 01

Define the Scope

We work with your team to identify the systems, features, and risk areas to target. No lengthy onboarding — a focused kick-off to set clear objectives and select the right tester profile.

STEP 02

Deploy the Network

We activate the right slice of our tester network — matched to your target user base by device, region, OS, and experience level. They test your product in real conditions, without assumptions.

STEP 03

Receive Clear Insight

You receive a structured, prioritised report — failure modes, usability risks, and edge cases ranked by severity. Larger engagements include a live debrief with your development team.
