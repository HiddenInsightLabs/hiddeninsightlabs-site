Section 02

Scope of the Pilot Evaluation

The Pilot Evaluation is a fixed-scope, introductory engagement. It includes:

  • Evaluation of one (1) end-to-end user flow within your app or software product, as specified by you at the time of booking
  • Testing conducted by a small, focused panel drawn from the Hidden Insight Labs tester network
  • A written findings report, including prioritised issues, severity ratings, and tester observations
  • One (1) results walkthrough meeting, scheduled at mutual convenience within 7 days of report delivery

The Pilot Evaluation does not include: automated testing, code review, re-testing after fixes, multiple flows, or any deliverable not listed above. Expanded coverage is available via our Essentials, Focused, or Standard engagement tiers.
